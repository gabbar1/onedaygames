Please follow below steps to deposit money to the OneDay wallet.

1. Open Google Pay/PhonePe / Paytm OR your netbanking.

2. Make payment of desired amount to click on above image

3. Note the Transaction ID OR Reference Number of the transaction.

4. Come back to the OneDay app >> Add Money and select the mode of payment which you used for the deposit money

5. Enter required details like your UPI ID / Account number-IFSC code, Money Amount and Transaction ID OR Reference Number of your transaction.

6. Your money will be reflected into your wallet in 2-3 hours during working hours (9 AM to 9 PM). If you deposit the money during OFF hours then it may take upto 2-14 hours depends upon how close your transaction time from the working hours.
